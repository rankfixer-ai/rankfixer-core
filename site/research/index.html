Methodology

How we calculate AI Visibility:

  • Schema Completeness (25%): Presence of JSON-LD, FAQPage, and Organization markup.
  • Entity Density (20%): Brand mentions and semantic clustering.
  • Content Answer Density (20%): Direct answers vs. marketing fluff.
  • Technical Signals (15%): Web Vitals, robots.txt AI-crawler access, and llms.txt implementation.
  • Backlink Quality (10%): Domain authority and trust flow.
  • Brand Entity Recognition (5%): sameAs links and knowledge base presence.
  • Freshness (5%): dateModified signals and content currency.
